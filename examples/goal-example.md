# G1: Publish weekly

## Goal
Publish 8 essays (≥800 words each) on my blog by 2026-09-29, one most weeks, each
shared in at least one community.

## Why (identity)
I'm a person who publishes, not a person who drafts.

## Anti-goals touched
Late nights (writing after midnight), no-new-projects (starting a newsletter
platform instead of writing).

## WOOP
- **Wish**: a real writing habit with published proof.
- **Outcome** (best case): 8 essays, two that people actually shared.
- **Obstacles**:
  1. Work crunch eats the writing block.
  2. Perfectionism: essay "not ready" for weeks.
  3. Novelty fades around week 5.
- **Plan**:
  | If | Then |
  |---|---|
  | Work crunch eats the block | Block moves to next morning, before email |
  | Essay not "ready" by Sunday | Publish it anyway at 80% |

## System

- Weekly scorecard measure: essay published this week? (yes/no) + cumulative n/8.
